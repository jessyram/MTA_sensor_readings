How is the air quality in the subway affected by Temperature?

Higher temperatures in the NYC subway make the air quality worse, by increasing pollutants like PM2.5 and humidity. Although these conditions are in effect during the summer, the winter season sees higher overall PM levels due to air getting trapped, while summer months have more active AC filtration
