What is TVOC

Total Volatile Organic Compounds (TVOC) is measurement of combined concentration of many different VOCs in a space; VOCs are gases from everyday items such as cleaning products, humans' exhaled breaths and personal care products, paints, varnishes, cleaning supplies, & construction materials. Levels above 2,200 ppb are extremely unhealthy and can lead to loss of coordination, liver/kidney damage, and short term effects can include fatigue and headaches.
